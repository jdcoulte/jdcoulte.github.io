About Me

I'm an investor and entrepreneur with over 10 years of experience buying, managing, investing, and divesting companies of all sizes. I spent 6 years in investment banking and private equity before leaving to invest my own capital. I invest across industries, mostly looking for overlooked and undervalued companies with proprietary technology. I'm also a self-taught full-stack developer. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with a BS in Business Administration in 2008.

As of July 2020 I live with my partner Mike and dog Boomer in Paris, France. We plan to move soon to Sydney, Australia once the Covid-19 pandemic has subsided. I'm an avid long-distance runner, having completed 19 marathons (and counting), with a personal best of 2:42:52 at the 2019 Donostia / San Sebastian Marathon in Spain. I love hiking and traveling to less-popular destinations. Ask me about my favorite trip!
